GREAT MINDS

Make creative thinking a habit.

Lots of people assume that creative thinking is arts and crafts. It's not.

Creative thinking is the ability to approach challenges and reframe them into possibilities. Even better, to come up with fresh and unexpected ways to solve them.

Why does it matter?

The only certainty is uncertainty.

For businesses now, creative thinking is key to success. The growth and survival of your business depends on innovation and creative thinking.

We all have creativity within us and innovation should be everybodys responsibility.

But, most companies rely on a small number of creatives to solve all these problems for them. If they do invest in creative thinking, they send a handful of people on a training course. Then, they expect them to come back with endless great ideas. This model doesn't work. Its expensive, distracting and doesn't deliver long term results.

To have creative confidence, you need to practice.

How often have you heard your team say Im not creative. Thats the root of the problem - creativity isnt a trait, its a mindset. You build it through experience and over time it becomes a behaviour. You can then lean on that behaviour when you are solving a problem, or being open to new opportunities.

We give you the tools and space to practice.

Great Minds is a creative thinking habit builder that fits into people's daily lives. It helps teams to develop creative thinking habits so they can drive change.

It makes sense for your business, but also for your team. The world of work is changing with more routine jobs being automated. Creative thinking can unlock new, fulfilling career paths.
